About Trust Signature

A secure and personalized verification system for your digital payments.

Why We Built the Trust Signature

Trust Signature is a secure desktop-based payment system designed to build confidence in online transactions. Inspired by real user feedback, it introduces a personalized digital signature that verifies each payment using encryption and real-time authentication. By combining transparency, familiarity, and security, Trust Signature ensures that every transaction feels safe, personal, and trustworthy.

Our solution introduces a personalized digital signature that provides a visual confirmation for each payment by utilizing encryption, as well as real-time authentication, making online transactions feel safer, more human, and most importantly, trustworthy.

Our Journey

We began this project with contstructing a secure login and banking connection. After this was set, we implemented the personalization of the user's digital signature and confirming identity. To further enhance the application, we created a full payment workflow with real-time updates and a verified digital receipt.

How It Works

  1. Sign in and connect your bank: Users link their preferred bank so that the system can verify their identity and retrieve contact details.
  2. Customize Trust Signature: Users can personalize their digital stamp by choosing the shape, color, and font.
  3. Make and verify a payment: The system provides real-time updates, progress animations, and visual confirmations of each step.
  4. Download a verified receipt: After completion, users can view, download, and share a digitally stamped proof of payment!

Why Desktop?

Based on user research, participants reported feeling the most confident when completing financial tasls on a desktop interface due to larger screens which enables more precise input control. For future expansion, we are planning on creating a mobile app for the Trust Signature.
